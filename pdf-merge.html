❓ FAQ
Is my sensitive private document architecture secure?

Completely. The data conversion engine builds binary buffer sets strictly via client-side scripts. No documents or text segments are ever uploaded to cloud databases, guaranteeing total legal document isolation.

Will merging large multi-page asset sets reduce reading resolutions?

Not at all. The processing algorithm maps out structural internal element dictionaries natively without recoding content streams. Vectors, distinct layers, text arrays, and embedded objects remain entirely lossless.

Can I merge password-encrypted document schemas directly?

For safety compliance protocols, our local canvas libraries require files to be decrypted first. If your PDF is password locked, remove the encryption block before running the multi-stitching workspace compiler loop.
