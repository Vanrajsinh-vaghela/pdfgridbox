❓ FAQ
Can I mix PNG and JPG files together in a single conversion?

Yes! The client engine handles PNG and JPG file compression signatures seamlessly. It parses binary header blocks locally to inject PNG nodes or raw JPEG bitstreams smoothly into the destination PDF layout.

Does converting images to PDF reduce overall photo clarity?

No. Selecting standard layout options applies mathematical vector scaling strictly to coordinate frameworks without re-compressing underlying image pixel density. Your source images retain original crispness and high resolution.

Is client-side local image conversion safer than cloud converters?

Absolutely. Traditional converters upload graphic files onto external database repositories where data logs may persist. Our converter operates entirely inside your browser RAM thread, ensuring complete data privacy.

How many images can I convert into a single PDF file at once?

There are no software-imposed limits. You can select dozens of images simultaneously. Total performance depends on your device's available system RAM memory.

Will my images be reordered during the PDF stitching process?

No. The system preserves the exact selection order of your uploaded files, compiling them sequentially from page one to the final page in your output PDF document.
