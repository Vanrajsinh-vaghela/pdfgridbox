❓ FAQ
Can I mix PNG and JPG graphics interchange files together?

Yes. The client engine handles both compression signatures interchangeably. It parses the binary header blocks locally to inject PNG nodes or raw JPEG bitstreams smoothly into the destination page layout object layer.

Will changing layout style compress the resolution clarity fields?

Not at all. The Standard A4 layout applies mathematical vector downscaling matrix values strictly to coordinate frameworks. The pixel density fields inside your source files remain completely uncompressed and high definition.

Why is client-side local conversion safer than cloud tools?

Traditional converters force you to upload graphic files into external database repositories, exposing you to privacy risks. This module operates strictly inside your browser RAM, guaranteeing absolute secure hardware data isolation.
