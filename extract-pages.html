❓ FAQ
Does page extraction compress or downscale internal images?

No. Our compilation matrix copies original data stream segments directly without decoding or modifying visual pixels. Your high-resolution charts and graphics look exactly as sharp as the original container asset.

What happens to active forms and annotations during isolation?

Since pdf-lib targets the low-level object tree array structures natively, it maintains interactive form data, metadata keys, and drawn comments on the isolated nodes perfectly, provided they aren't bound to missing elements.

Is there an absolute limit on file sizes for browser-level rendering?

Our tools are totally unrestricted on the software end. The only bounds are your own device's CPU and available RAM memory allocations. Large files up to several hundred pages stream fluidly on standard modern computers.
