Why Add Page Numbers to PDF Files Privately?

Numbering multi-page PDF documents is essential for legal filings, academic papers, research journals, and business reports. Online tools that require cloud uploads can expose your sensitive contracts or financial statements to unauthorized server logging. Our client-side pagination utility adds page numbers to PDF files online free with complete privacy. By processing document byte arrays directly inside your web browser's RAM, your corporate documents and personal records remain 100% private and localized to your hardware device.

Custom Placement, Formatting & Title Cover Exclusion

Our tool gives you complete layout flexibility over your PDF pagination. Choose from 6 strategic placement positions including Bottom Center, Bottom Right, Top Center, or Top Right. You can select clean numbering formats like standard digits ("1, 2, 3") or full context strings ("Page X of Y"). Furthermore, the "Skip Front Cover" feature automatically bypasses index zero, ensuring title pages and cover sheets stay clean without unnecessary page numbers.

Ultra-Sharp Vector Rendering & Font Scale Preservation

Unlike basic image stamp utilities that reduce document clarity, our engine injects page numbers directly into the PDF's native vector object tree using standard embedded PostScript fonts (Helvetica). This guarantees that numbers remain ultra-sharp at any zoom level or high-resolution printout without altering text readability, hyperlinks, or underlying document layouts.

Will injected page numbers cover dense background graphics or text?

No. The mathematical drawing engine registers text blocks at top layer object index configurations. Generated page numbers render cleanly over existing visual background layers.

Does the page numbering process break interactive hyperlinks?

Not at all. Low-level binary structure arrays remain completely untouched. The layout algorithm simply references standard Helvetica packages to draw clean text nodes, leaving internal anchor paths 100% functional.

Is there a total page limit constraint for large documents?

No. Client-side architecture scales fluidly across documents containing hundreds of pages. Execution speed depends entirely on your device's available RAM and processor power.

Can I start page numbering from a specific page number?

Yes. By enabling "Skip Front Cover", numbering starts cleanly on the second page (page index 2) while keeping your cover title sheet completely unnumbered.

Are my PDF files uploaded or saved on any remote server?

No. All pagination operations run locally inside your browser thread memory. Your original files and output documents are never uploaded, stored, or logged on remote servers.
